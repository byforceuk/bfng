Privacy

Privacy policy.

BFNG collects only the information needed to operate missed-call recovery and respond to enquiries. This page is a launch-ready plain-English policy and should be reviewed by a qualified professional before heavy traffic or paid ads.

Information collected

BFNG may collect business contact details, caller numbers, job details, postcodes, urgency, callback status, SMS history and enquiry messages where needed to operate the service.

How information is used

Information is used to send missed-call text-back messages, capture job enquiries, notify the trade business, manage callback reminders, produce missed-job reports and respond to BFNG sales/support enquiries.

Data sharing

BFNG does not sell customer or caller data. Service providers may process data where required for SMS, call routing, hosting, analytics or form delivery.

Retention

Lead and call data should be retained only as long as needed for service operation, reporting, support and legal/accounting requirements.

Contact

For privacy enquiries, use the contact details listed on the BFNG website or enquiry form.
