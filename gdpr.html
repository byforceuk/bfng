GDPR

UK data handling notes.

This page is designed to raise trust before reviews exist. It explains how BFNG thinks about customer and caller data. It should be reviewed before formal launch.

Practical data minimisation

BFNG should only capture information needed for missed-call recovery: caller number, job type, postcode, urgency, message content, callback status and reporting fields.

Purpose limitation

Caller data should be used to help the trade business respond to the enquiry, manage callbacks and understand missed-job recovery. It should not be sold to third parties.

SMS handling

Text messages should be transactional and directly related to the caller’s attempted contact with the trade business.

Access and deletion

Customers should be able to request reasonable access, correction or deletion of relevant stored lead data where lawful and practical.

Processor relationships

Telephony, SMS, hosting and form providers may process data on BFNG’s behalf. Their privacy and processing terms should be checked before production use.
