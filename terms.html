Terms

Terms of service.

These terms are written for the current BFNG positioning: paid setup plus monthly missed-call recovery support. They should be legally reviewed before formal launch.

Service

BFNG provides missed-call text-back setup, job-capture forms, callback reminder configuration, simple lead tracking and missed-job reporting for trade businesses.

Upfront setup

The setup fee covers routing checks, SMS writing, form build, reminder configuration, test calls, handover and post-install checks.

No free trial

BFNG does not offer a free trial by default. Prospects can inspect the demo flow, audit tool, setup checklist and example reports before buying.

Install-work guarantee

If BFNG cannot install a working missed-call text-back flow around the customer’s phone setup, the setup fee is not payable or should be refunded if already paid. This guarantee covers installation work, not revenue results.

Monthly subscription

The monthly subscription covers ongoing service access, monitoring, reporting, light edits and support as described in the chosen plan.

Customer responsibility

The trade business remains responsible for calling leads back, marking job outcomes where needed, complying with its own customer obligations and keeping contact details accurate.
