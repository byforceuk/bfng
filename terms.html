Service

Upfront setup

The setup fee covers routing checks, SMS writing, form build, reminder configuration, test calls, handover and post-install checks.

48-hour install guarantee

If BFNG cannot install the agreed missed-call text-back flow within 48 hours of receiving the required setup details, the setup fee is refunded. This guarantee covers installation timing, not revenue results.

Monthly subscription

The monthly subscription covers ongoing service access, monitoring, reporting, light edits and support as described in the chosen plan.

Customer responsibility

The trade business remains responsible for calling leads back, marking job outcomes where needed, complying with its own customer obligations and keeping contact details accurate.
