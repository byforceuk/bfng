Why plumbers need this

Plumbers lose leads when the first response is silence.

A plumbing enquiry is often immediate: water is coming through a ceiling, a boiler has failed, or a blocked toilet needs action. If the call is missed, the customer usually keeps searching until someone responds.

BFNG sends an instant reply, asks for the right job details, and keeps the callback visible so a serious plumbing enquiry does not disappear into a phone log.
