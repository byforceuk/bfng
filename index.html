The system

A trade business does not lose enquiries in one place. It loses them across the whole contact flow.

Most trade websites focus on getting someone to call. BFNG focuses on what happens after the customer tries: missed calls, slow callbacks, weak forms, quote requests with no follow-up, and no clear record of which enquiries are still alive.

01

Capture

Missed callers receive an instant branded text. Website visitors can submit job details through a short quote/callback form.

02

Qualify

Customers are prompted for postcode, job type, urgency, photos and preferred callback time so the team can prioritise properly.

03

Track

Every enquiry sits in a simple board instead of disappearing into a phone log, inbox, notebook or WhatsApp thread.

04

Follow up

Callback reminders, quote follow-up templates and review request flows help staff keep the sales process moving.

FAQ

Questions before installation.

Is BFNG a marketing agency?

BFNG is positioned as an enquiry recovery and job capture system. It can support marketing activity, but the core package focuses on capturing, tracking and following up enquiries more reliably.

Is this only for businesses that need more work?

No. Busy businesses may need better filtering, tracking and follow-up. Quieter businesses may need stronger capture and conversion. The audit determines which problem is real.

Does the package guarantee jobs?

No. BFNG does not guarantee booked jobs. It installs the infrastructure needed to reduce leakage from missed calls, poor forms, manual callbacks and weak follow-up.

Can this be connected to AI receptionist or CRM tools later?

Yes. The system is designed as a foundation. AI receptionist, deeper CRM, ads, booking and reporting upgrades can be scoped after the recovery layer is working.

Why charge a four-figure setup fee?

The setup fee covers audit, page/form build, text flow, tracking board, templates, testing, handover and launch support. The monthly fee covers support, reporting, small edits and maintenance.
