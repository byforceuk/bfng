FAQ

Questions before installation.

Is BFNG a marketing agency?

BFNG is an enquiry recovery and job capture system. It can support marketing activity, but the core package focuses on capturing, tracking and following up enquiries more reliably.

Does the package guarantee jobs?

No. BFNG installs the infrastructure needed to reduce leakage from missed calls, poor forms, manual callbacks and weak follow-up.

Can AI receptionist features be added later?

Yes. The system is designed as a foundation. AI receptionist, deeper CRM, booking and reporting upgrades can be scoped after the recovery layer is working.

Why charge a setup fee?

The setup fee covers audit, page/form build, text flow, tracking board, templates, testing, handover and launch support.
