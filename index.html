What is missed call text back?

Missed call text back is an automatic SMS reply sent when a customer calls your trade business and you cannot answer. For tradespeople, the important part is speed. A customer with a leak, fault, lockout, roof issue or drainage problem may not wait for voicemail. BFNG replies quickly, asks for the job details, and gives you a cleaner callback lead.

Why trades lose jobs from missed calls

Many trade jobs are urgent or time-sensitive. A missed call from a homeowner can turn into a booked job for someone else within minutes. BFNG is built for plumbers, electricians, roofers, locksmiths and other trades who cannot always stop work to answer the phone.

Missed call text back vs AI receptionist

An AI receptionist can answer calls, qualify enquiries and book jobs. That is powerful, but it is not always the best first step. BFNG starts with the simpler leak: missed calls. Once the missed-call recovery flow is proven, AI qualification and voice answering can be added later.

Missed call text back vs human call answering

Human call answering can work well, but it may be more cover than a small trade business needs at the start. BFNG gives tradespeople a lower-friction first layer: instant text-back, job capture, callback reminders and reporting.

FAQ

Common questions from tradespeople.

Can BFNG work with my current trade number?

The site now positions this as existing-number routing guidance rather than promising every setup is identical. The sales call should confirm whether the trade uses mobile, VoIP, landline forwarding or another call setup.

Does this replace voicemail?

It does not need to remove voicemail. It makes the first response active instead of passive. The caller gets an immediate SMS and can reply with job details before they call someone else.

Why charge an upfront setup fee?

The upfront fee covers installation, message writing, form creation, routing checks, lead board setup and test calls. The monthly fee covers support, reporting, reminders and small improvements.

Is this only for emergency trades?

No. It is strongest for urgent trades, but it also works for builders, landscapers, cleaners, pest control, heating engineers and any service business where inbound calls become jobs.

When should I add AI receptionist features?

After the missed-call text-back flow is catching real enquiries. BFNG should use AI later for qualification, call answering, quote follow-up and booking once the simple recovery system has proven value.
