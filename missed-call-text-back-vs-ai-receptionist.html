Comparison guide

Missed call text back vs AI receptionist.

AI receptionists can be powerful, but they are not always the best first step for a small trade business. BFNG starts with the simpler leak: missed callers who already wanted to speak to you.

Missed call text back

Best when the immediate problem is that callers get no response while the trade is on-site. It is simpler to understand, cheaper to run and easier to install than full AI call answering.

AI receptionist

Best when call volume is high enough to justify automated answering, qualification, booking and call summaries. BFNG treats this as a later upgrade path.

BFNG position

Start by recovering missed calls. Capture the job. Remind the trade to call back. Report what happened. Add AI when the missed-call layer is already working.
