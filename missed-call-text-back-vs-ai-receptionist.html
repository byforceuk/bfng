Comparison guide

Missed call text back vs AI receptionist.

AI receptionists can be useful, but they are not always the cleanest first step for a trade business. BFNG starts with the simpler leak: customers who already tried to contact you and got no response.

Missed call text back

Best when the immediate problem is silence after a missed call. It is easier to understand, easier to install and easier for the customer to trust than full AI call answering.

AI receptionist

Best when call volume is high enough to justify automated answering, qualification, booking and call summaries. BFNG treats this as an upgrade after the basic capture layer is working.

BFNG position

Start by recovering missed calls. Capture the job details. Keep the callback visible. Report what happened. Add AI only when the missed-call and follow-up layer is already working.
